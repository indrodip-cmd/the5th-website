Indrodip Ghosh, Founder

I grew up surrounded by women who spent decades helping others — families, workplaces, communities, entire industries — and rarely got paid what they were actually worth.

Later, as a marketer and funnel strategist, I kept seeing the exact same pattern. Women with 20, 30, even 40 years of expertise who genuinely believed they had nothing worth selling. Meanwhile, people with a fraction of their experience were building profitable online businesses.

That disconnect built The5th. Not as a course. Not as another coaching program with a Slack group. As a real system with real coaching and a real community — designed specifically so that the next woman who feels like she's starting from zero realises she's actually starting from everything.
