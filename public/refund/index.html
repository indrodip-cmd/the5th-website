Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Refund Policy explains how refunds and guarantees work across our programs. It should be read together with our Terms of Use and the specific Client Enrollment Agreement you sign when you enrol.

The signed Client Enrollment Agreement for your program always governs the exact guarantee and refund terms for that program. Where it differs from this page, the signed agreement prevails.

1. Fast Forward (Private Group Coaching)

Fast Forward is a premium, application-based coaching program backed by a written guarantee. As documented in your signed Client Enrollment Agreement:

  • We work with you to reach $10,000 per month within the 6-month program term, provided you attend the weekly calls, complete the work, and follow the system.
  • If you have not reached that result, we continue working with you at no additional cost for up to 12 months until you do.
  • If, after 12 months of full participation, you have not generated at least 2× your investment, we refund 100% of your investment.

The guarantee is conditional on your full participation. Because this is a high-touch program with limited seats, fees are otherwise non-refundable except as set out in your agreement or as required by law.

2. The Collective (Membership)

The Collective is an annual membership protected by a 365-day money-back guarantee: show up to the live coaching and work the method for the year, and if you do the work and don't see results, we refund your membership. Full terms are presented at enrolment.

3. The5th AI (Subscription)

You may cancel a The5th AI subscription at any time to stop future billing. Fees already paid for the current billing period are non-refundable except where required by applicable law.

4. Digital Products & Resources

Because digital products are delivered immediately and cannot be returned, they are non-refundable once accessed, except where a specific guarantee applies or where required by law.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund or discuss your guarantee, email us at indrodip@10kroadmap.org with your name, the program, and the reason for your request. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

6. Chargebacks

If you have a billing concern, please contact us first so we can resolve it. Initiating a chargeback without contacting us may delay resolution and affect your access to the Services.

7. Your Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy limits any non-waivable rights you may have under applicable consumer-protection law, including any statutory cooling-off or cancellation rights.

8. Contact Us

Questions about refunds? Contact us at indrodip@10kroadmap.org.
