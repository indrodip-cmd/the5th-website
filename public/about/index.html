Founder portrait
My Story

I wasn't born into abundance. I built it.

I grew up in a lower-middle-class family in India, where every rupee was counted twice. My parents broke into their savings to put me through college. No wealthy relatives. No business network. No shortcuts.

Like most people, I thought a degree would be enough. It wasn't. What followed were years of trying, stumbling, and starting again, until I became obsessed with understanding business, psychology, marketing, sales, and how people really make decisions.

That obsession became my career. Today I've advised Fortune 500 executives, billion-dollar companies, celebrity coaches, government organizations, and entrepreneurs around the world.

But the title I'm proudest of isn't "consultant." It's helping ordinary people build businesses they never believed were possible.

Lifestyle / coaching photo
Beyond Business

Strategy is half of it. The rest is who you become.

I've met extraordinary people, many of them sharper than those earning ten times their income. Their problem was never talent. It was marketing, sales, and believing they were allowed to charge what they were worth.

I know that feeling. That's why I built this, not to teach complicated theory, but to help good people build businesses that create real freedom.

Beyond business, I'm committed to personal growth, emotional resilience, lifelong learning, my health, and a life with the freedom to travel, contribute, and spend real time with the people I love.
