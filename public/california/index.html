California Privacy Rights

Last updated: July 2026

This notice supplements our Privacy Policy and applies to residents of California. It describes the rights you have under the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (together, the "CCPA"), and how to exercise them with The5th Consulting ("we", "us", or "our").

Terms not defined here have the meaning given in our Privacy Policy. If there is any conflict between this notice and the Privacy Policy on matters of California privacy rights, this notice controls for California residents.

1. Personal Information We Collect

In the past twelve months, we have collected the following categories of personal information from California consumers. We collect this information directly from you, automatically as you use our site, and from service providers acting on our behalf.

2. How We Use This Information

We use the categories above for the business purposes described in our Privacy Policy, including to provide and deliver our programs, process payments and enrolments, communicate with you, send marketing where permitted, improve and secure our Services, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sale and Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information for money. However, our site uses third-party advertising and analytics technologies (for example, through cookies) that may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising under the CCPA. You can opt out of these technologies at any time.

We do not knowingly sell or share the personal information of consumers under 16 years of age.

4. Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

To opt out of the use of cookies and similar technologies for advertising and analytics, open your privacy settings and turn off the Marketing and Analytics categories, then save your choices:

Your Privacy Choices → Manage Cookie Settings

We also honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where required. Because these preferences are stored on your browser and device, you may need to renew your choices if you clear cookies or use a different browser or device.

5. Your California Privacy Rights

Subject to certain exceptions, California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to know, the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources, the purposes, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to delete, request that we delete the personal information we have collected from you.
  • Right to correct, request that we correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to opt out, opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information as described above.
  • Right to limit, limit the use of sensitive personal information (note: we do not use sensitive personal information for purposes that trigger this right).
  • Right to non-discrimination, we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

6. How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit a request to know, delete, or correct, contact us at indrodip@10kroadmap.org with "California Privacy Request" in the subject line. To opt out of "sale" or "sharing," use the Manage Cookie Settings link above, no account is required.

We will verify your identity before responding, typically by confirming information we already hold about you. You may designate an authorized agent to make a request on your behalf; we may require proof of the agent's authorization and verification of your identity.

7. Shine the Light

California Civil Code Section 1798.83 permits California residents to request certain information about our disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. We do not disclose personal information to third parties for their own direct marketing.

8. Changes to This Notice

We may update this notice from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent version. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.

9. Contact Us

Questions about your California privacy rights? Contact us at indrodip@10kroadmap.org.
